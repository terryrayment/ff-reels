# Homepage — `/site`

## Goal

First impression. A visitor arrives knowing nothing about Friends & Family and within ten seconds understands: who we are (a commercial production company), what tier we're at (the work loads first), and who's on the roster (directors visible).

## Direction

Currently director-led. Per the Canada lean, push **toward project-first** without abandoning the director surface — open with a reel/hero, then a project grid, with directors as the third scroll moment.

## Proposed structure (top → bottom)

1. **Hero** — full-bleed video. One muted, looping highlight reel or featured film. Minimal overlay copy.
2. **Recent work** — 6–8 project thumbnails, no titles on hover (titles appear underneath, small).
3. **Directors** — 6 director cards, hover-to-play.
4. **Quiet footer link to Archive / Work / About / Contact**.

This flips the current scaffold's order (directors before work). Project-first is the Canada move.
