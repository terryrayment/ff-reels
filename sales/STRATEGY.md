Condensed working version. Full strategy lives at `~/Documents/CommercialProductionSalesReps_Research_20260513/west-coast-sales-strategy.md`.

## Positioning

> F&F × CCCo. is the only West Coast commercial rep partnership built around director authorship, talent access, and integrated craft — not bid-list volume.

## The four wedges

### 1. Director-who-could-be-a-film-director (auteur tier)
**For:** W+K LA, 72andSunny, Mother, Anonymous, MullenLowe, RPA, Quality Experience, Gut, Doner, JKR.
**Deliver:** Curated director-monograph emails. LBB / SHOOT placements. Festival entries (AICP, Cannes YDA, Ciclope, 1.4).

### 2. Talent pipeline you don't have (Gunder's moat)
**For:** Brand-direct + smaller agencies needing recognizable on-screen talent fast.
**Deliver:** Director + talent attached packages. Quarterly "talent thinking" doc to 20 senior agency producers + 10 brand leads.

### 3. Integrated craft package (Chiara's structural advantage)
**For:** Auto launches, tech product films, fashion, beauty, gaming — design-and-motion-heavy briefs.
**Deliver:** F&F director × Union editor × Brand New School/Laundry designer pre-aligned packages.

### 4. Founder in the room (Terry's edge)
**For:** Senior agency producers + ECDs tired of reps who can't decide and EPs who don't show.
**Deliver:** One LA week per month, 12+ meetings/trip. Founder dinners every 8 weeks, 5–6 producers + 1 F&F director.

## 90-day plan (CCCo. West Coast onboarding)

- **Days 0–14:** Exit Uncle Lefty cleanly. Diligence Gunder's LA bandwidth.
- **Days 14–45:** Parallel-process Reber Covington, Rogue Rep, Carolyn Reps, VLM as comparison set. Audit roster conflicts.
- **Days 45–60:** Decide. Onboard. Trade press announcement.

## What we are NOT doing

- Competing for high-volume social/content briefs (in-house agencies + AI win there)
- Chasing every triple bid (33% theoretical win = 5–10% real)
- Out-LA'ing LA-native reps in year one
- Letting Gunder front commercial-craft pitches without Chiara backing
- Trying to be on every bid list

## The one thing

> Find the brief where being F&F × CCCo. is the only credible answer — and own it.
