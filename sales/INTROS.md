# Founder Intro Email Library — 60 Prospects

Each email follows the same architecture: one specific observation, one creative idea or director-reference, one small ask, one weird signoff. Voice: Terry as founder, wholesome lunatic, no jargon. Subject lines under 6 words. Plain text, no signature block bigger than 4 lines.

**Before sending each email:** verify the recipient on LinkedIn, confirm their last campaign or post, swap in any specific detail you've found, replace cinema-director references with F&F directors who actually shoot that way.
