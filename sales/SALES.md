- Triage LinkedIn: 3 thoughtful comments on prospects' posts.
- Check rep pipelines: one rep ping per day rotating.

**Weekly:**
- 6 cold outreach emails (per send plan above).
- One agency-producer touch outside any active bid (NYC or LA).

**Monthly:**
- 5-day LA week (12+ meetings, 1 founder dinner).
- Director-by-director performance review.
- One trade-press placement push.

**Quarterly:**

- Rep relationship review (90/180/365 day cadence).
- Festival entries + awards strategy.
