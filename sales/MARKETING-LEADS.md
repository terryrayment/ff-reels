## How to use this list

1. **Verified (9):** ready to email today. Match the founder pitch tone but lead with the work and the production model, not the partnership.
2. **Unverified (9):** acceptable to send to the pattern-guessed address — bounce risk is real but small. Or warm Apollo up monthly: many of these (Andrei Najjar, Anisha Raghavan, Aila Morin) are likely to surface as Apollo refreshes.
3. **Don't dual-send same week.** Either founder OR marketing lead first, then wait 10 days before the other contact at the same company.
4. **Project board:** every name in this doc has a corresponding `MKT #N` item in the FF Sales project board with verified/unverified status reflected.
