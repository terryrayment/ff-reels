Spot Notes — Matt Dilmore

"Gifts" · KFC · Wieden+Kennedy · 2024 · 0:30

"Gifts." We shot this in July on a stage in Toronto — fake snow, a rented Christmas, and a nine-year-old who'd never been asked to be disappointed on cue. The whole spot lives on her face when she realizes the bucket isn't a toy, so we protected half a day for that one reaction. No line reads. I just told her the truth: sometimes the best gift is fried chicken.

— Matt Dilmore, Director
